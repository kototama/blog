* Tips

** Increase scrollbar size in Firefox

#+begin_src

about:config

# for the scrollbar width

widget.non-native-theme.scrollbar.size.override 30

# for the scrollbar shape

widget.non-native-theme.scrollbar.style 4

#+end_src
